Today's Wisdom:

Who

I am Joey to some, Joe to others, and Joseph on my driver's license. I began life as a son, but have also filled roles such as brother, friend, musician, student, and many others. Most recently I became a graduate of the pure math department at Berkeley.

What

This is my first website ever, so the premise is simple: a curation of quotes and excerpts I find meaningful from books I read. I sort them by theme, not genre, because I think it's more useful. Other than that, there's no rhyme or reason to the ordering.

When

I started reading as a little kid. I started collecting quotes in college. I started sharing them (on this site) in 2020.

Where

I was born in Korea, raised in Pennsylvania, then went to college in California. I try my best to find book recs from conversation, but have found many great recs from Literary Hub, n+1, and of course reddit.

Why

For the sake of permanence - these are the souvenirs I collect from books. For the sake of keeping sharp. I am inspired by many people, but this site was inspired by a few people I love: Jerry, Tim, Lois, and Trent.
